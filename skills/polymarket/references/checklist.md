# Polymarket research checklist

Use this checklist when evaluating a market:

1. Exact market title / question
2. Exact resolution source and wording
3. Expiry / resolution date
4. Current implied probability
5. Recent catalyst or news
6. Main arguments for YES
7. Main arguments for NO
8. Liquidity / market quality concerns if visible
9. What would change the view quickly
10. Final short conclusion
